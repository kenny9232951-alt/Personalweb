About Me

Empathy meets execution.

"Not just a dreamer. Not just a operator. Someone who has walked the ground, done the work, and left the data to prove it."

01 · World as Classroom
The World Is My Classroom

Visited 15+ countries, with a particular fondness for the ones that make people ask "you're really going there?" Real understanding only begins when your feet touch the ground — not from books, not from reports.

02 · Storytelling as Tool
Story Is My Strongest Tool

I believe every person carries a story worth telling. My work is to collect those stories and translate them into curricula, reports, scripts, or a talk that changes how someone thinks.

03 · Integration as Expertise
My Expertise Is Finding Connections

Theatre training gave me empathy. Data analysis gave me precision. I'm not a specialist in one field — I'm the person who finds market strategy in theatre theory, and GTM gaps in field research.
